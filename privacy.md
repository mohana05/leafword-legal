# LeafWord Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 23, 2026
**Last updated:** April 23, 2026

## The short version

LeafWord is built for families. We designed it so **your child's information never leaves their device**. We do not run servers, we do not collect analytics, we do not show ads, and we do not share anything with third parties.

If you only read one section, this is it.

---

## Who we are

LeafWord is developed and maintained by Mohan ("we", "us"). You can reach us at [mohan.a05@gmail.com](mailto:mohan.a05@gmail.com).

## What information LeafWord stores — and where

Everything LeafWord creates lives **on your device only**, inside the app's private sandbox. We (the developer) never receive it.

**Stored locally on the device:**
- Your child's display name and age band (as you enter them)
- A daily word, its meaning, example sentence, and a short fun fact (generated on-device by Apple Intelligence)
- Your child's recall progress (which words they've remembered or want to practice)
- Streak counts and weekly progress indicators
- App preferences (notification time, voice choice, on-device AI toggle, reading pace)

**Never transmitted to us:**
- Your child's name
- Your child's learning progress, streaks, or any lesson content
- Any identifiers, device data, or usage analytics
- Your email, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone data, or location

We genuinely do not operate a server for LeafWord. There is nowhere for this data to go.

## How LeafWord uses Apple Intelligence

LeafWord uses **on-device Apple Intelligence** (via Apple's `FoundationModels` framework) to generate each day's word, meaning, example sentence, and fun fact. This processing happens entirely on your iPad or iPhone — no prompts, responses, or generated content are sent to Apple's servers or to us.

Apple's handling of its on-device language model is covered by Apple's own privacy policy. See [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy).

## How LeafWord handles subscriptions

LeafWord offers an optional **Family Plus** subscription (monthly or annual) via Apple's App Store. When you subscribe:

- Your payment is handled by **Apple** using your Apple ID — we never see or store your payment information.
- Apple tells our app whether your subscription is active, but does not give us your personal purchase details.
- Any subscription receipts, refund requests, and payment records are governed by Apple's own policies. See Apple's [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) and [Terms and Conditions](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/).

## Notifications and on-device speech

- **Local notifications** (daily word reminders) are scheduled on your device. No push notification service is used, and no notification content leaves your device.
- **Read-aloud (text-to-speech)** uses Apple's built-in speech synthesizer on your device. Nothing is sent over the network.

## No analytics, no tracking, no ads

LeafWord includes **zero** third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or user tracking. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Sentry, Crashlytics, or any comparable tool. We do not participate in Apple's advertising program.

## Children's privacy (COPPA)

LeafWord is intended for children ages 4–12, used under parental supervision.

Because LeafWord does not transmit or collect any personal information from children (or adults) to our servers, we are not a COPPA "operator collecting personal information from children online" in the traditional sense. All child-provided content stays on the device.

**Parental controls:**
- A parental gate (math challenge) protects Settings, subscription management, and any external links.
- Parents can review the child profile at any time via Settings → Profiles.
- Parents can delete any child profile, all learning history, and all subscription data directly on the device; nothing persists elsewhere.

If you believe your child has entered sensitive information in the app and you would like guidance removing it, contact us at [mohan.a05@gmail.com](mailto:mohan.a05@gmail.com). Because we do not receive any data, the most effective removal is on the device itself.

## For users in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, our **lawful basis** for the minimal on-device processing described above is **your consent**, expressed by installing and using the app under parental supervision.

Because we do not receive or store personal data, most GDPR rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection, restriction) can be fully exercised by using the app's own Settings (edit, delete profile, export data). If you need additional help, contact us.

## For users in California (CCPA / CPRA)

We do not sell or share personal information as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act, and we do not receive personal information from you to begin with. California residents have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt out — all of which are fully possible because nothing leaves your device.

## Data security

Information stored on your device is protected by iOS's standard sandbox and file-protection mechanisms. Subscription status is verified by Apple using signed receipts. Because nothing leaves your device, there is no server for an attacker to target.

## Exporting and deleting your data

- **Export:** Settings → Privacy & Data → Export data produces a JSON file on your device containing profiles, daily words, and recall history. The file is yours; nothing is uploaded.
- **Delete a profile:** Settings → Profiles → tap profile → Delete.
- **Delete everything:** Uninstall LeafWord. iOS will remove the app sandbox, including all profiles, words, streaks, and preferences. There is no remote copy anywhere.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change how LeafWord handles data, we will update this page and update the "Last updated" date at the top. If a future change ever introduces data transmission off the device, we will prompt you inside the app for explicit consent before it takes effect.
