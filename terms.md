## 2. What LeafWord does

LeafWord is a daily-word vocabulary app. Each day, Apple Intelligence (running on your device) generates a word appropriate for your child's age band, along with a kid-friendly meaning, example sentence, and short fun fact. LeafWord also provides:

- Multiple child profiles (in the same family)
- A recall/practice system for previously learned words
- A weekly "garden" view of progress
- On-device text-to-speech for word and sentence pronunciation
- Optional daily reminder notifications
- An optional **Family Plus** subscription for additional features
